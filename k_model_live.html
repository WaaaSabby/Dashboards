Season wt — how much the model weighs a pitcher's full-season K% vs his last-30-days K%: K% used = wt × season + (1−wt) × last-30
0.70 (default) — season leads, recent form nudges. The sane setting.
Lower (0.50–0.60) — trusts recent form more. Use mid-season when L30 is the truer signal (velo bump, new pitch, role change).
Higher (0.85–1.0) — nearly ignores L30 (1.0 = pure season K%). Use early season when 30-day samples are tiny, or when one blowup is skewing L30.
Applies to all pitchers — change it and hit Run slate to re-project. Season K% is stable but slow to catch surges/fades; L30 catches form but is noisy (5–6 starts). The blend balances both.
