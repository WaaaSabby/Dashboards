Season wt — how much the model weighs a player's full-season rates (RBI/G, HR/G, SB/G) vs his last-30-days rates: rate used = wt × season + (1−wt) × last-30
0.70 (default) — season leads, recent form nudges. The sane setting.
Lower (0.50–0.60) — trusts recent form more (hot bat, lineup promotion, green-light stretch).
Higher (0.85–1.0) — nearly ignores last-30 (1.0 = pure season). Use early season or when a small sample is skewing L30.
Applies to all players — change it and hit Run slate to re-project.
